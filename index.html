About

Fight Fusion is an exhilarating game designed to fuel your passion for combat and strategy. In this high-paced game, players engage in rounds of intense fighting, utilizing a combination of skills and tactics to outmaneuver their opponents. Each round lasts for 10 seconds, and players must win more than 2 rounds to claim victory. If there's a draw in the initial rounds, extra rounds are added until a winner emerges. The game pits players against the computer, making each match a unique challenge. Get ready for a thrilling experience that tests your reflexes, strategy, and endurance!
